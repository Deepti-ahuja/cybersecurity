Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks. It is important because it protects all categories of data from theft and damage. Cyber security is a subfield of computer science.

Steps you must take to ensure a safe Cyber Space


1- Go offline, when internet is not required.
2- Update your security software regularly.
3- Shut down your laptop, when not in use.
4- Don't click untrusted links.
5- Keep your privacy settings on.
6- Avoid public wi-fi.
7- Don't give your personal information on insecure websites.
8- Don't download unsafe files.
9- Make your passwords strong.

How to be CyberSafe and CyberPeaceful?

In the course of tech evolution, many branches of technology appeared, one of which is (say) Artificial Intelligence (AI). There are many serious threats online nowadays. To prevent these, use an anti-virus software. Before transporting any external files in your device, check for virus scans. Isolate or delete the file in case of any suspicion. Clear your cache and cookies regularly for a better experience. Awareness in young and innovative minds about the menace of a fully technological world must be spread. So it’s time to be Judicious and act sagaciously.
